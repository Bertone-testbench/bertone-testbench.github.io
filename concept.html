Why AI tutoring works

Independent education research shows that well-designed AI tutors can match — and sometimes exceed — traditional tutoring, when they are built to teach, not just answer.

➜

Intelligent Tutoring Systems can match human tutors in learning outcomes, outperforming standard classroom instruction in multiple large studies.

➜

Generative AI improves tutoring quality by enabling natural dialogue, adaptive explanations, and dynamic questions based on what students actually say and write.

➜

Students learn better in psychologically safe environments, where they can ask questions freely without fear of judgment or embarrassment.

➜

Design matters more than technology: effective AI tutors use hints, scaffolding, and step-by-step guidance instead of giving instant answers.

Source adapted from: Mary Burns, Consultant — Brookings Global Task Force on AI in Education
